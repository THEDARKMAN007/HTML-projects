Corned Beef Spaghetti Recipee
ingredients
Spaghetti, oil, knor,cube, onion, green pepper, carrot, cornbeef

Cooking Directions
  1. Boil half pack of Spaghetti and add little salt.
  2. Diced carrot 3medium size, diced spring onions 3,diced white onions, a spoon of oil, grinded dry pepper, tomato paste, 1knorr cube, salt to taste and corn beef.
  3. Fry the above, starting with white onions, add other veggies, Knorr cube, tomato paste mixed with little water, salt, and grinded pepper, fry for 3minutes.
  4. Then add corn beef and mix before adding drained spaghetti
  5. Serve with boiled egg.
