WHO ARE WE?



ORIGIN
Thewanderers.com is community of passionate travellers founded by Adams Abdulsamad, CEO of multi-billion dollar company Avatars.
He created this community in december 2021 as a little website project, and since then the community have grown to a size of 10 million active members


VISION
The goal of our community is to explore the magnificent areas of the world, especially for passionate wanderers who hate staying in one place all their lives.


ACHIEVEMENTS
We make travelling easier and less stressful and help to minimize the cost of such hobby.
We have agents and guides all over the world that woukd make your travelling experience worthwhile
